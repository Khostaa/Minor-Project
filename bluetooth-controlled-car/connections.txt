## Components Used
- Arduino
- Motor driver
- Battery

~ On 2 sides of motor driver - connect a wire from each of motors.

~ Connect Pins (as specified in code) of motor driver to arduino.
~ S/C the Enable Pins for maximum output.
~ Connect +12V and GND of motor driver to the battery.
~ Take Output from +5V and GND to supply power to  Arduino.

(with Arduino connect to PC - Commands can be given to car using serial monitor by specifying letters as in CODE.)
